About Me

My interest in cybersecurity started young — watching action films where technology was the weapon, the key, and the solution. That curiosity led me to Computer Science in high school and eventually across the world to UWE Bristol, where I've spent three years turning that excitement into real skills.

Originally from Jakarta, Indonesia, studying abroad was the best decision I ever made. It taught me resilience, independence, and the courage to face discomfort — lessons no classroom could fully teach.

To me, cybersecurity is both protection and exploration — defending what matters, understanding how things break, and staying curious in a world that never stops changing.

I'm not just building a career. I'm building a life with no borders.
